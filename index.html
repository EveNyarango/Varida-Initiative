Varida Initiative

About us

Varida Initiative; is a digital platform aimed at enabling smallholder farmers gain better access to market linkage. Through this program farmers will be linked buyers who buy goods in bulk either for retail sales
or value addition such as supermarkets, schools, hotels, restaurants, exporters, processors and manufacturers.
Moreover farmers exploitation will be eliminated as observed in the picture at the middle especially when it comes to pricing and quantities. Socially the initiative will work on positively impacting the youths to part of the agribusiness sector in Kenya and contribute towards the big 4 agenda in promoting food security in the country.

Services Offered;

  1. Market linkage; we are offering an online platform that support farmers to sell in bulk at fair prices and quantities.
  2. Agribusiness Consultancy; our team is available to advise you on Good Agricultural Practices.This will increase yield resulting to increased income
  3. Financial Literacy training services; we are empowering farmers to do farming as a business. We train them how to minimize cost of production and maximize income. Finally how to manage better the acquired income through budgeting, savings, debt management and insurance.
  4. Youth and women empowerment; Socially we are advocating for youth and women to be part of agribusiness sector and contribute to something greater in food security.
